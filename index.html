The Challenge

Revenue data shouldn’t be this hard to trust

Most SaaS companies run revenue through multiple systems — billing platforms, payment processors, CRMs, accounting tools. Each one tells a slightly different story. When these numbers don’t match, the impact ripples across the business: financial closes take longer, audits get complicated, and leadership makes decisions on data they can’t fully trust.

Our early research suggests that these discrepancies are not edge cases. They’re structural, widespread, and often invisible until they compound into material gaps.
